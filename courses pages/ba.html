Online BA Course Syllabus

Explore a wide range of liberal arts subjects across six semesters:

Semester 1 & 2
  • Sem 1: English Literature I, History of Ancient Civilizations, Intro to Sociology, Political Science I, Fundamentals of Psychology
  • Sem 2: English Literature II, Medieval History, Social Psychology, Political Science II, Developmental Psychology
Semester 3 & 4
  • Sem 3: Modern English Lit, Modern World History, Cultural Anthropology, Political Thought, Research Methodology
  • Sem 4: Postcolonial Literature, Contemporary World History, Urban Sociology, International Relations, Abnormal Psychology
Semester 5 & 6
  • Sem 5: Literary Theory, Gender Studies, Environmental Sociology, Public Administration, Counseling Psychology
  • Sem 6: Comparative Literature, Human Rights, Sociology of Religion, Political Economy, Organizational Psychology
