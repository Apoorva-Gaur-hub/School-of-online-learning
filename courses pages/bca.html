Online BCA Course Syllabus

The curriculum is divided into 6 semesters covering foundational and advanced IT subjects:

Semester 1 & 2
  • Sem 1: Intro to Programming, Computer Fundamentals, Mathematics, English Comm, Environmental Studies
  • Sem 2: Data Structures, DBMS, Object-Oriented Programming, Discrete Math, Digital Electronics
Semester 3 & 4
  • Sem 3: Operating Systems, Web Programming, Software Engineering, MIS, Data Communication
  • Sem 4: Computer Networks, Database Administration, Cloud Computing, Mobile Programming
Semester 5 & 6
  • Sem 5: Cyber Security, AI, IoT, Big Data Analytics, Project Work (Part 1)
  • Sem 6: Machine Learning, Blockchain Technology, Business Intelligence, Project Work (Part 2)
