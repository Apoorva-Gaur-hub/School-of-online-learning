Online B.Com Learning Syllabus

A comprehensive 6-semester breakdown covering business, finance, and accounting:

Semester 1 & 2
  • Sem 1: Financial Accounting, Business Economics, Business Math, Principles of Management, Communication
  • Sem 2: Corporate Accounting, Microeconomics, Business Statistics, Marketing Mgmt, Business Law
Semester 3 & 4
  • Sem 3: Cost Accounting, Macroeconomics, Management Accounting, Income Tax Law, HRM, E-Commerce
  • Sem 4: Advanced Corp. Accounting, Financial Mgmt, Research Methodology, Indian Economy, Auditing, Elective I
Semester 5 & 6
  • Sem 5: Corporate Governance, Banking & Insurance, Business Ethics, International Business, Strategic Mgmt
  • Sem 6: Project Management, Financial Markets, Investment Mgmt, Entrepreneurship, Dissertation
