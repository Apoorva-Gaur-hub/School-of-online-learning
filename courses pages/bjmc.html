Online BJMC Course Syllabus

General syllabus overview for the 3-year Online BJMC degree program:

Semester 1 & 2
  • Sem 1: Introduction to Journalism, Communication Skills, Basic Computer Skills
  • Sem 2: Print Journalism, Media Laws & Ethics, Photojournalism
Semester 3 & 4
  • Sem 3: Television Journalism, Radio Journalism, Media Research
  • Sem 4: New Media, Public Relations, Advertising
Semester 5 & 6
  • Sem 5: Media Management, Feature & Opinion Writing, Documentary Filmmaking
  • Sem 6: Media Criticism, Digital Media Production, Internship/Project
