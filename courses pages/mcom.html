Online M.Com Course Syllabus

Each semester consists of subjects related to accounting, taxation, and finance. Here is the semester-wise breakdown:

Semester 1
  • 1. Organizational Behavior
  • 2. Managerial Economics
  • 3. Advanced Statistics
  • 4. Marketing Management
  • 5. Computer Apps in Commerce
Semester 2
  • 1. Corporate Laws
  • 2. Financial Management
  • 3. Indian Financial System
  • 4. Management of Nonprofit Org
  • 5. Tax Planning and Management
Semester 3
  • 1. Advanced Financial Management
  • 2. Entrepreneurship Development
  • 3. IT and E-commerce
  • 4. Advertising
  • 5. Sales Management
Semester 4
  • 1. International Business Env.
  • 2. Corporate Accounting
  • 3. Operations Management
  • 4. Project Work
  • 5. Dissertation
