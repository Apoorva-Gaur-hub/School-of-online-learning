Online MJMC Course Syllabus

A comprehensive two-year program covering news reporting, ethics, and digital media:

Semester 1
  • 1. Intro to Journalism & MC
  • 2. Media Laws and Ethics
  • 3. Reporting Techniques
  • 4. Communication Skills
Semester 2
  • 1. Print Journalism
  • 2. Electronic Media
  • 3. New Media
  • 4. Media Research
Semester 3
  • 1. Media Management
  • 2. Advanced Reporting
  • 3. Media and Public Relations
  • 4. Documentary Filmmaking
Semester 4
  • 1. Media Criticism
  • 2. Global Media Dynamics
  • 3. Dissertation/Project Work
  • 4. Internship
