For Indian Students

UG Programs
  • 10th Class Certificate (10 years of formal schooling)

PG Programs
  • Graduation in any discipline with 40% marks minimum
  • Understanding of the English Language is required

For Foreign Students

UG Programs
  • O Level Certificate (10 years of schooling required)
  • A Level Certificate (12 years of schooling required)
  • Equivalence from AIU is mandatory. Refer AIU
PG Programs
  • Must have completed a recognized graduation degree
  • AIU equivalence certificate is mandatory. Refer AIU
  • Experience: Some programs need 1–2 years work exp

FAQs - Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. Are these online degrees UGC-approved? +

Yes. Universities partnered with SOOL are UGC-entitled and authorised to offer online degrees under the UGC (Online Programmes) Regulations.

Q2. Is an Online MBA equal to a Regular MBA? +

Yes, if the degree is from a UGC-approved university, it is legally equivalent. Career value depends on the university’s reputation and course quality.

Q3. How are the exams conducted? +

Exams are conducted online in AI-proctored mode with ID verification, webcam monitoring, and timed assessments to ensure authenticity.

Q4. What is the typical fee range for programmes? +

Generally ₹90,000 – ₹2,00,000 for complete programmes. UG: ₹80,000 – ₹1.5 Lakh, PG/MBA: ₹1 – ₹2 Lakh (Varies by university and specialisation).

Q5. Are loans or EMI options available? +

Yes. Most universities provide semester-wise payment plans, No-Cost EMI options, and in some cases bank-linked education loans or scholarships.

Q6. Is placement assistance provided after completion? +

Yes. Most universities offer placement support and career counselling. Through SOOL’s partnerships, students gain access to broader career opportunities.
