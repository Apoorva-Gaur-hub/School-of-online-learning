About Shoolini University Online

Shoolini Online University is part of Shoolini University, a young, research-driven, and multi-disciplinary private university established in 2009 by leading academicians and professionals. Shoolini University has quickly become one of India’s top 100 universities (NIRF) and is recognized globally for its research, innovation, and patents.

The university combines academic excellence with social responsibility, aiming to create knowledge and contribute to community development. All courses are UGC-DEB-approved and offered by an NAAC A+ accredited institution, ensuring credibility. The online platform benefits from internationally qualified faculty, innovative teaching methods, and a strong focus on career-oriented learning and placements.

Admission Process for Shoolini University Online

Follow these steps to apply for online courses at Shoolini University for 2026:

Step 1: Online Registration: Visit the official portal, complete the form, and pay the registration fee.

Step 2: Eligibility Check: Admission is based on academic merit in the qualifying examination.

Step 3: Document Upload: Submit scanned copies of marksheets, certificates, and ID proof.

Step 4: Verification: Eligible candidates receive an official admission offer letter after review.

Step 5: Enrollment: Pay the program fee to receive login credentials for the LMS.
