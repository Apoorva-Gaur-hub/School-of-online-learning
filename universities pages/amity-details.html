About Amity University Online

Amity University Online is a pioneering initiative by Amity, dedicated to providing individuals with a robust platform for elective online learning. This initiative empowers participants to enhance their competencies, cultivate expertise, and refine skills across diverse disciplines and career paths. The program allows students to study at their own pace while still receiving a high-quality education that connects to real-world situations. Students learn from experts who work in their field, work together with other students, and practice what they learn through real examples and hands-on projects.

Amity University Online provides a state-of-the-art learning management system (LMS) that allows students to access course materials, submit assignments, and participate in discussions from anywhere in the world. The program doesn’t just teach job skills – it also teaches students how to be good leaders, make the right choices, and communicate well with others. This helps create complete professionals who can handle the challenges of today’s work world.

Last Date of Admission in Amity University Online

Amity Online offers admission twice a year. The first round starts in January, and the second round happens in July. The entire admission process, from registration to final confirmation, is done online. There is no entrance exam required to apply.

Admission Process for Amity University Online

Here’s how you can apply for online courses at Amity University for 2026:

Step 1: Visit the official Amity Online website (keep in mind there are separate websites for online and on-campus programs).

Step 2: Click on the "Apply Now" button at the top right corner.

Step 3: Fill in your basic personal and academic details. If you have a job, include that information as well.

Step 4: Upload the required documents, including proof of eligibility and a recent passport-sized photo.

Step 5: Submit the application form and pay the application fee. After review, the university will send you an enrollment email.
