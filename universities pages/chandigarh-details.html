About Chandigarh University Online

Chandigarh University Online (CU), which was established in 2012 in Mohali, Punjab, is dedicated to advancing research, innovation, and multidisciplinary education. This university features itself as one of the topmost institutions in India. Chandigarh University Online is accredited by the UGC – University Grants Commission and, in fact, is awarded an esteemed A+ grade from the NAAC – National Assessment and Accreditation Council.

CU’s Online Education is designed to offer a result-oriented curriculum, providing various undergraduate, postgraduate, and Phd courses. It offers both affordability and accessibility for students. CU online programs are designed to prepare students with in-demand skills required in today’s competitive market. Chandigarh University online offers quality learning resources, interactive online platforms, and even personalised guidance from reputed faculty members.

Last Date for Chandigarh University Online Admissions

CU online admissions is underway right now; students can observe changes in dates with each session. Chandigarh University Online has two main sessions – January to June and July to December. Students are advised to apply before the closing date. The admission process is entirely digital.

CU Online Admission Process

The process is easy, simple, and can be done digitally from anywhere by following these steps:

Step 1: Application Form: Students must fill in their personal, academic, and work details online.

Step 2: Fee Payment: Next step is to pay the fee for the 1st semester, year, or full program.

Step 3: Document Upload: Upload needed documents such as mark sheets, ID proof, and so on.

Step 4: University Approval: The university will review and verify the details before approving admission.

Step 5: Session Start: Once approved, students can access CU online courses and study materials.

Step 6: Support Access: Students can get help through digital platforms throughout their program.
