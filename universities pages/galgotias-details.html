About Galgotias Online University

Galgotias Online is a pioneering initiative by Galgotias University, dedicated to providing individuals with a robust platform for elective online learning. This initiative empowers participants to enhance their competencies, cultivate expertise, and refine skills across diverse disciplines and career paths. The Online degree program allows students to study at their own pace while still receiving a high-quality education that connects to real-world situations.

Galgotias Online provides a state-of-the-art learning management system (LMS) that allows students to access course materials, submit assignments, and participate in discussions from anywhere in the world. The program doesn’t just teach job skills – it also focuses on developing leadership qualities and ethical decision-making, creating complete professionals ready for today's work world.

Last Date of Admission in Galgotias Online

Galgotias Online offers admission twice a year. The first round starts in January, and the second round happens in July. The entire process is digital, and there is no entrance exam required to apply.

Admission Process for Galgotias Online

Follow these steps to apply for online courses at Galgotias University for 2026:

Step 1: Visit the official Galgotias Online portal.

Step 2: Click on the "Apply Now" button at the top right corner.

Step 3: Fill in basic personal, academic, and work details (if any).

Step 4: Upload required documents (eligibility proof & photo).

Step 5: Submit form and pay the application fee. You will receive an enrollment email after review.
