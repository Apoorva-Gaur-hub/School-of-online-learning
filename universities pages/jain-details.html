Last Date of Admission in Jain University Online

Jain Online offers admission twice a year. The first round starts in January/February, and the second round happens in July/August. The entire Jain University course admissions process, from registration to final confirmation, is done online. There is no entrance exam required to apply.

Admission Process for Jain University Online

Here’s how you can apply for online courses at Jain University for 2026:

Step 1: Visit the official Jain Online website (keep in mind there are separate websites for online and on-campus programs).

Step 2: Click on the "Apply Now" button.

Step 3: Register yourself with your basic details for verification of email and mobile number.

Step 4: After email verification, you will receive login details. Log in and fill out the online enrollment form.

Step 5: Upload the required documents, including proof of eligibility and a recent passport-sized photo.

Step 6: Submit the application form and pay the application fee. After your application is reviewed and your documents are confirmed, the university will send you an email with details about your enrollment and the next steps.

Important Requirements:
  • According to the latest UGC guidelines, all candidates must create their ABC ID (Academic Bank of Credits) and DEB ID to get admission.
  • Students can enroll twice a year – in July/August and January/February sessions.
