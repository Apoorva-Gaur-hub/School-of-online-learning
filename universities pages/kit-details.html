About KIIT Online University

KIIT University, established in 1992 in Bhubaneswar, is one of India’s leading universities, known for academic excellence and global recognition. KIIT Online is the digital learning platform offering UGC-DEB approved online programs. The platform combines advanced technology with interactive learning, enabling students to access live sessions, tutorials, eBooks, and practice assignments through a user-friendly LMS.

KIIT University emphasizes industry-relevant skill development, allowing students to study at their own pace while balancing work and education. With flexible learning options, credible programs, and a strong support system, KIIT Online is shaping the future of higher education in India by enhancing employability and career growth.

Last Date of Admission in KIIT Online University

The last date to apply for admission at KIIT Online University is mid-October. Students are advised to complete their registration before the deadline to secure their seats and ensure timely access to the LMS and academic support services.

Online Admission Process for KIIT University Online Courses

The process at KIIT Online is student-friendly and ensures a smooth journey:

Step 1: Online Registration – Register on the portal, pay ₹1450 registration fee, and upload mandatory color documents.

Step 2: Document Verification – The admission team verifies documents within 48 hours to confirm eligibility.

Step 3: Program Fees Payment – Once confirmed, pay the program fees to activate LMS access within 2 days.

Step 4: Other Fees – Note additional charges for project evaluation, transcripts, or specialization changes if applicable.

Step 5: Orientation Session – Attend an online orientation to familiarize yourself with the LMS and course structure.

Step 6: Final Enrollment – An enrollment number is issued, and you become officially enrolled as a student.
