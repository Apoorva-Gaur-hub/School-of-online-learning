Last Date for Lovely Professional University Course Admissions

LPU Online University course admissions take place twice a year. The first academic cycle starts in January and lasts until March. However, the second admission cycle starts in July and lasts till October. The entire admission process from registration to final enrollment is fully online. Students do not need to appear for any entrance exam to apply for Lovely Professional University courses.

Admission Process for Lovely Professional University Online

The admission process at LPU Online University is simple and fully digital. It allows students to apply from anywhere. All Lovely Professional University course admissions are completed online without the need for an entrance exam. Here are the steps to apply:

Step 1: Start Applying – Click on the Apply Now tab on the official LPU Online website.

Step 2: Registration – Fill in all the details and get your student dashboard.

Step 3: Application and Fee Payment – Complete the application form and make the fee payment online.

Step 4: Document Submission – Upload required documents and confirm your admission.

Important Requirements:
  • According to the latest UGC guidelines, all candidates must create their ABC ID (Academic Bank of Credits) and DEB ID to get admission.
  • Students can enroll twice a year – in July/August and January/February sessions.
