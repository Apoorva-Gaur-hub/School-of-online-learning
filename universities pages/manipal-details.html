Manipal Online University Admission Process

The Manipal Online University admission process is simple and fully digital. Students can apply for Manipal Online Courses from anywhere by following these steps:

Step 1: Application Form: Fill in your personal, academic, and work details online.

Step 2: Fee Payment: Make admission fee payment for the first semester, year, or full program.

Step 3: Document Upload: Upload required documents like mark sheets, ID proof, and certificates.

Step 4: University Approval: The university reviews and verifies your details before confirming admission.

Step 5: Session Start: Once approved, students get access to Manipal Online Courses and study materials.

Step 6: Support: Learners receive help through digital platforms and guidance throughout their program.
