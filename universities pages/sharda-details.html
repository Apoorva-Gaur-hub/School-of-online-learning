About Sharda Online University

Sharda Online is a pioneering initiative by Sharda University, dedicated to providing individuals with a robust platform for flexible online learning. This initiative empowers participants to enhance their competencies, cultivate expertise, and refine skills across diverse disciplines and career paths. The online degree program allows students to study at their own pace while still receiving a high-quality education that connects to real-world situations. Students learn from experts who work in their field, work together with other students from around the world, and practice what they learn through real examples and hands-on projects.

Sharda Online provides a state-of-the-art learning management system (LMS) that allows students to access course materials, submit assignments, and participate in discussions from anywhere in the world. The program doesn’t just teach job skills – it also teaches students how to be good leaders, make the right choices, and communicate well with others in a multicultural environment. This helps create complete professionals who can handle the challenges of today’s global work world.

Last Date of Admission in Sharda Online

Sharda Online offers admission twice a year. The first round starts in January, and the second round happens in July. The entire admission process, from registration to final confirmation, is done online. There is no entrance exam required to apply for Sharda admission.

Admission Process for Sharda Online

Here’s how you can apply for online courses at Sharda University for 2026:

Step 1: Visit the official Sharda Online website (keep in mind there are separate websites for online and on-campus programs).

Step 2: Click on the "Apply Now" or "Admission" button on the homepage.

Step 3: Fill in your basic personal and academic details. If you have a job, include that information as well.

Step 4: Upload the required documents, including proof of eligibility and a recent passport-sized photo.

Step 5: Submit the application form and pay the application fee. After review, the university will send you an enrollment email.
