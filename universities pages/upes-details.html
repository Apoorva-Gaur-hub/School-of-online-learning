About UPES Online University

UPES Online is one of the prestigious universities for online learning. Established in 2003, it is now UGC-entitled, NAAC-accredited with an A grade, and has secured a 36th NIRF ranking in the Management Category. These recognitions guarantee its credibility and the validity of the degree. The university offers various specialised programs in undergraduate, postgraduate, and certification programs.

UPES Online ensures that students gain skills to excel in their careers with flexibility and affordability. The university has esteemed and experienced faculty from IIT, IIM, and IVY league. These online degree courses are updated, industry-aligned, and designed to match your career goals.

Last Date for Online UPES University Admissions

The deadline for UPES University Online admissions is arriving soon. For the January batch, the registration deadline is 20th July, while for the July batch, the deadline is 20th January. Late fees may apply after these dates.

Online UPES University Admission Process

Getting admitted to UPES University Online is easy! Follow these steps:

Step 1: Sign Up: Go to the UPES Online website and fill in your details like name and contact information.

Step 2: Pick Your Program: Choose the program that matches your interests and qualifications.

Step 3: Upload Documents: Upload your important documents like certificates and ID proof.

Step 4: Pay the Fee: Pay the application fee through the website before the last date.

Step 5: Interview & Selection: If needed, the university will guide you through the interview process.

Step 6: Confirmation: After selection, you will receive your admission confirmation.
